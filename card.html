Giant Panda

Giant pandas are bears that are native to China, where they are considered a national treasure.

  • Diet: Bamboo (They eat bamboo 12 hours a day)
  • Habitat: Mountainous regions of central China, in Sichuan, Shaanxi and Gansu provinces
  • Size: 4 to 5 feet (1.2 to 1.5 meters) tall and weigh up to 300 lbs. (136 kilograms)
  • Clasification: Giant pandas are indeed bears

Also called great pandas, parti-colored bears, bamboo bears and white bears, giant pandas are distinguished from other pandas by their large size and black-and-white coloring. The bold coloring may provide camouflage, according to the Smithsonian National Zoo.
